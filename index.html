****About Us****



Welcome to More Blessings Academy, where excellence meets opportunity in the heart of Kempton Park, Gauteng. For over a decade, we've been dedicated to providing the best in curriculum-based education alongside a rich array of sporting and extracurricular activities. At More Blessing, we believe in nurturing not only the academic talents of our students but also their holistic development. That's why we offer a comprehensive curriculum designed to inspire curiosity, critical thinking, and creativity in every child. Our experienced faculty members are committed to providing personalized attention to each student, ensuring that they reach their full potential academically and beyond. But education isn't just confined to the classroom at More Blessing.




Our Lovely Teachers changing our lives


We understand the importance of fostering a well-rounded individual, which is why we offer a wide range of sporting and extracurricular activities. From athletics to arts and everything in between, there's something for everyone to explore and excel in. In addition to our academic and extracurricular offerings, More Blessing is proud to have produced quality students and graduates who have gone on to excel in their chosen fields. Our alumni network is a testament to the success of our educational philosophy and the dedication of our staff. At More Blessing, we believe that education should be accessible to all.
That's why we welcome students from all backgrounds and walks of life, ensuring that everyone has the opportunity to thrive in our supportive learning environment.



For those who require it, we also offer boarding facilities, providing a safe and comfortable home away from home for our students. And when it comes to dining, our exquisite diet ensures that every student receives nutritious and delicious meals to fuel their learning and growth.



Join us at More Blessing Private School and discover a world of endless possibilities where every child is empowered to reach for the stars.
