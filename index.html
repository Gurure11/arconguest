About Us

Arcon Guest House is located in Vanderbijlpark, next to the Vaal Institute in the heart of the Vaal area. We offer beautiful and affordable overnight accommodation and restful day stays for travelers, professionals, and families.

Our rooms range from Double Rooms with shared bathrooms to En-suite Double Rooms, as well as Single Rooms with double beds. Day rest options are also available starting from just R150 for 3 hours.

We are a self-catering guest house with everything you need for a comfortable stay. Enjoy our beautifully equipped kitchen with modern appliances, perfect for preparing your own meals at your convenience.
